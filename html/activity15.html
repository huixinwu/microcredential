🔙Activity 15: AJAX

For the following activity, you will use the fundamentals of AJAX to create an web-app that will display facts about cats. The facts are obtain from https://catfact.ninja website.

The web-app should show in a division different cat facts when a button is clicked. It also has a Clear and Reset button that when is clicked it resets and clears the fact in the division
