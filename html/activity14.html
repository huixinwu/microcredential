🔙Activity 14: jQuery fundamentals

For the following activity, create two events using jQuery to two different figures, a circle and a square. The events are:

  • click event: when the square shape is clicked, its background color changes randomly and its text displays the number of times it has been clicked.
  • mouseleave event: when the mouse moves outside the circle, its background color randomly changes and the text shows the number of times the mouse has moved out of it.
